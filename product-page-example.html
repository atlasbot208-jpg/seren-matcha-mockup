About Haru

Meet Haru, our debut ceremonial blend from Uji, Kyoto - the birthplace of matcha. Hand-picked in the first harvest, shade-grown for 29 days, and stone-milled slow, it's crafted for balance: a soft creamy morning cup with vivid umami that also holds its own in a latte.

Named after the Japanese word for "spring," Haru captures the gentle awakening of the season - fresh, bright, and full of quiet energy.

Origin & Craftsmanship

  • Region: Uji, Kyoto, Japan
  • Harvest: First flush (shincha season)
  • Cultivars: Saemidori & Yutakamidori
  • Shading: 29 days under traditional coverings
  • Processing: Hand-picked, hand-sorted
  • Milling: Granite stone-milled at low speed

Cultivar Details +

Saemidori: Known for its vibrant green color and sweet, mellow flavor with minimal astringency. Produces a creamy, umami-rich matcha.

Yutakamidori: Adds depth and complexity with a slightly fuller body. Contributes to Haru's signature creaminess and long, sweet finish.

Blending these two cultivars creates a balanced matcha that's both approachable for newcomers and satisfying for matcha enthusiasts.
