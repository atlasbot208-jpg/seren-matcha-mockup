Meet Haru - our debut ceremonial blend from Uji, Kyoto, the birthplace of matcha.

Hand-picked in the first harvest, shade-grown for 29 days, and stone-milled slow, Haru is crafted for balance: a soft creamy morning cup with vivid umami that also holds its own in a latte.

Named after the Japanese word for "spring," Haru captures the gentle awakening of the season - fresh, bright, and full of quiet energy.

ORIGIN & PROCESS +

Region: Uji, Kyoto, Japan

Harvest: First flush (shincha season)

Cultivars: Saemidori & Yutakamidori

Shading: 29 days under traditional coverings

Processing: Hand-picked, hand-sorted

Milling: Granite stone-milled at low speed

HOW TO PREPARE +

Traditional: Sift 2g into bowl, add 60ml water (70-80°C), whisk in M motion until frothy.

Latte: Prepare as above, then add 250ml steamed milk.
