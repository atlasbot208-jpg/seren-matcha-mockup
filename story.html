The Third Wave of Matcha

Coffee had its third wave. Now it's matcha's turn.

We believe matcha is more than just a trendy green drink. It's a ritual, a reset, a movement. Just like third-wave coffee reshaped café culture with quality and transparency, we're pioneering the third wave of matcha in Australia.

Our tencha leaves are sourced with care from small family-run farms in Uji, Kyoto - but they're made for here and now. For shelves in Sydney apartments, Melbourne cafés, Brisbane mornings, and everywhere in between.

Clean design. Bold flavour. Tools that make the ritual effortless.

Whether you're swapping coffee for calm focus or just curious to try something new, Seren makes matcha simple, modern, and part of everyday Australian life.
