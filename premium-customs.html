Customs & International Shipping

Australian Customers

All matcha sold within Australia has already cleared customs and meets Australian Border Force requirements. No additional customs fees or duties apply to Australian orders.

International Shipping

We ship premium matcha internationally to select countries. Please note:

  • International orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes
  • Customers are responsible for all customs fees in their destination country
  • Delivery times vary by destination (typically 7-14 business days)
  • Tracking is provided for all international shipments

Countries We Ship To

Currently shipping to:

  • Australia (domestic)
  • New Zealand
  • Singapore
  • United States
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom

Customs Declaration

All international shipments are declared as "Green Tea Powder (Matcha)" with accurate value. We do not mark items as gifts or undervalue shipments, as this is illegal and can result in shipment delays or confiscation.

Estimated Customs Duties by Country

New Zealand: Generally duty-free for tea products under NZD $1,000

United States: Tea is duty-free, but state sales tax may apply

Canada: Tea is generally duty-free, GST/PST may apply

United Kingdom: May be subject to VAT and customs duty

Singapore: GST applies to imported goods

*These are estimates only. Please check with your local customs authority for current rates.

Shipment Delays or Issues

If your international shipment is delayed or held at customs, you may need to:

  • Contact your local customs office for clearance
  • Provide additional documentation (invoice, product description)
  • Pay applicable duties and taxes before release

We're here to help! Contact us at serendipityaustralia2024@gmail.com if you need customs documentation or assistance.

Import Restrictions

Matcha (green tea powder) is generally permitted in most countries. However, some regions have specific requirements or restrictions on imported food products. We recommend checking your country's import regulations before ordering.
