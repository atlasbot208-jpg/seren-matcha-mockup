The Third Wave of Matcha

Coffee had its third wave. Now it's matcha's turn.

We believe matcha is more than just a trendy green drink. It's a ritual, a reset, a movement. Just like third-wave coffee reshaped café culture with quality and transparency, we're pioneering the third wave of matcha in Australia.

Our tencha leaves are sourced with care from small family-run farms in Uji, Kyoto - but they're made for here and now. For shelves in Sydney apartments, Melbourne cafés, and everywhere in between.
